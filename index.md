# About Me
---

I am currently an PhD Candidate with at the School of Electronic Engineering, Xidian University, under the supervision of Prof. Chunna Tian. Prior to that, I received my B.E. from the School of Electronic Engineering, Xidian University in 2018. My research interest is computer vision, currently with emphasis on vision-and-language model. I am also interested in Image-to-text and Text-to-image generation tasks. 

<p><b> E-mail: yxding@stu.xidian.edu.cn </b></p>
